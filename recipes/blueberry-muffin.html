Blueberry Muffins

Description

Enjoy this fruitful, sweet, breakfast desert recipe to start off your mornings

Ingredients

Muffins:

  • All-purpose flour
  • White sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • One egg
  • Fresh blueberries

Topping:

  • White sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Mix wet ingredients in a liquid measuring cup.
  3. Add wet mixture to dry mixture, then fold in blueberries.
  4. Make the topping.
  5. Pour the batter into a prepared muffin tin and sprinkle with the topping.
  6. Bake the preheated oven until the toothpick comes out clean.
