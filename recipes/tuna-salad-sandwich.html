Tuna Salad Sandwich

Description

Simple tuna salad sandwich recipe that can be prepared in around 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tuna in water, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup mayonaise or salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 8 slices bread

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the tuna, celery, onion, mayonaise, lempn juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread tuna mixture on 4 bread slices. Top with remaining bread slices.
